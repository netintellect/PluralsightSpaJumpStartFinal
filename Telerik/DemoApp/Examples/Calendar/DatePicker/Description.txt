Telerik DatePicker control is a fully featured data-input control providing the ability to select dates from a calendar displaying different views, 
like months, years, decades, centuries, multiple months view etc. Additionally, the DatePicker control provides an advanced mechanism that can parse
numbers and strings to dates. Entering "Monday" or "mon" will select Monday of the current week for an "English" cultured DatePicker, whereas 
upon entering 3, the DatePicker will choose the 3rd day of the current month.

RadDatePicker is a part of the <b>Telerik.Windows.Controls.Input</b> assembly.
