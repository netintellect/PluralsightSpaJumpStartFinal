<b>MinimumRangeSpan</b> determines the minimum value distance between RadSlider's SelectionStart and SelectionEnd properties.
This means that if SelectionStart is 4 and MinimumRangeBase is 2, then SelectionEnd cannot be LESS than 6.
<br/>
On the other hand <b>MaximumRangeSpan</b> is the maximum value distance between RadSlider's SelectionStart and SelectionEnd properties.
This means that if SelectionStart is 4 and MaximumRangeBase is 2, then SelectionEnd cannot be MORE than 6.
