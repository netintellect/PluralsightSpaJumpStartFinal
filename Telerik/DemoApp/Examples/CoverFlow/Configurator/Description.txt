Telerik CoverFlow turns navigation through a media collection into a dazzling visual experience. You can configure the CoverFlow's appearance by using several properties exposed for that purpose to let you easily determine the appearance and positioning of the control and its items in a straightforward manner. You can control the <b>vertical camera position</b>, <b>vertical center</b> of the CoverFlow and the items' <b>reflections</b>.

RadCoverFlow is a part of the <b>Telerik.Windows.Controls.Navigation</b> assembly.
