The <b>Docking</b> control exposes properties for the <b>Compass</b> and the <b>RootCompass</b> styles that allow you to customize thoose elements. Both elements expose properties, that allow you to show/hide some of there parts - the left, top, right, bottom and center indicators. In this example the <b>Compass</b> doesn't have center indicator, that doesn't allow you to drop multiple panes in the same group. The <b>RootCompass</b> in this example has hidden top indicator that doesn't allow you to dock anythig on the top part of the <b>Docking</b> control.
