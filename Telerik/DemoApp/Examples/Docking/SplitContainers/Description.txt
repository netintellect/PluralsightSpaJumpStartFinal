The SplitContainer allows you to use movable bars to divide the displayed area into resizable parts. With Telerik Docking's <b>SplitContainers</b> you can build complex layouts that can even include nested split areas. Some immediate examples of such layouts are Explorer-like and Outlook-like interfaces. The Docking's <b>SplitContainers</b> allow you to define the <b>Orientation</b> and the <b>InitialPosition</b> of the conrainer.
