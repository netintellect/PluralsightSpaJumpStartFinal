Telerik MaskedInput controls allow you to enter text, numbers and dates that conform to a given mask. The MaskedInput controls accept string and numeric data in various customizable formats and provide cross-culture support. The above example shows all the pre-defined masks and formats Telerik MaskedInput controls support.
