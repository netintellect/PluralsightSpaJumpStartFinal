<b>Indicators</b> are scale elements that point to a value along a scale. Linear scale can contain four types of indicators:

<b>Marker</b> - a small shape that can be located around scale bar.
<b>Bar</b> - a colored bar drawn around scale bar. One of ends of this bar points to the scale value.
<b>State Indicator</b> - a shape with arbitrary size located anywhere on the scale.
<b>Numeric indicator</b> - shows indicator's value as a number.
